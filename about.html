About Benjamin

Entrepreneur, photographer, and investor out of Minneapolis. I believe in building systems, betting on hard assets, and documenting the whole ride.

I run multiple ventures — a branding agency, a real estate practice, a photography studio, and a lawn care company — and I share everything I learn along the way. The through-line in all of it: the fundamentals of business are the same everywhere. Find customers, serve them well, follow up, repeat.

Off the clock you'll find me behind a camera, on a one-way flight, in the gym before sunrise, or deep in a conversation about Bitcoin, markets, and where the world is heading.
